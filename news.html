Louth Civic Trust | Heritage Open Days Free Walk

19 Sep 2019

On Friday 13th and Sunday 15th September the Louth Civic Trust hosted walks led by Mrs Mary Abbot, Dr Richard Gurnham and Mr James Laverack, to celebrate Louth’s rich architectural heritage and history as part of the Heritage Open Days National Festival.
